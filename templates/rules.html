Rules & Regulations

Guidelines to ensure a peaceful and productive environment for all residents

Chatrawas Rules

Please read and follow all rules carefully to maintain harmony in our community

General Rules

  • Every student must be pre-registered in the official register book and ensure details are accurate.
    Failure to do so may result in consequences as decided by the management.
  • Entry after 9:00 PM is not allowed without prior permission. Inform in advance if late return is expected.
    Repeated disregard may lead to restrictions or disciplinary action.
  • No outsider is allowed without permission. Every entry must be recorded with valid reason in the entry register.
    Violation may lead to suspension of entry privileges.
  • Maintain a quiet and focused environment at all times. Use headphones during calls, videos, or music.
    Disturbances may lead to warnings or restrictions.

Room Rules

  • Keep your room clean and organized daily. Each student must take responsibility for keeping their own space clean.
    Neglect may lead to compulsory cleaning duties.
  • Do not move furniture or room items without mutual understanding.
    Disagreements or issues may lead to intervention by the managing group.
  • Cooking is allowed in the kitchen only when required, with prior discussion to maintain cleanliness and order.
    Misuse may result in temporary kitchen access restrictions.
  • Weekly outdoor cleaning drives will be held. Participation is compulsory.
    Non-participation may lead to loss of certain shared privileges.

Safety & Security

  • Smoking, alcohol, and any illegal substances are strictly prohibited within the premises.
    Violation will result in immediate removal from the premises.
  • Before leaving, check all electrical appliances and gas connections. Do not leave heavy electric items running when not in use.
    Negligence may lead to loss of device usage privileges.
  • Every student is responsible for reporting unknown entries or unusual activity.
    Failure to report may be treated as a shared responsibility.
  • Make sure to log any guestâ€™s name and purpose in the entry register.
    Unauthorized access may result in restrictions on inviting guests.

Visitor Policy

  • Visitors must write their name, address, contact number, and reason for visit in the entry register.
    Unrecorded visits may lead to limited visitor permissions.
  • Only common areas can be used for meeting visitors.
    Violations may affect future visitation allowance.
  • No visitor entry allowed after 7:00 PM.
    Late visits may be denied entry at the gate.
  • Students must immediately report any misbehavior or issue caused by a visitor.
    Delays in reporting may result in review of visitor privileges.

About Us

Nilratran Chatrawas provides comfortable and affordable accommodation for students pursuing education in nearby colleges.
