About Our Chatrawas

Comfortable Living for Students

Nilratran Chatrawas, an initiative by Keshav Smarak Samiti, is dedicated to offering affordable and secure accommodation to students arriving from different parts of the country for their education.Guided by the values of service and discipline, we provide clean and peaceful living spaces equipped with essential facilities—so that students can concentrate on their academic goals in a supportive environment without concerns about lodging

Our Facilities

We provide all essential amenities to make your stay comfortable and productive

High-Speed Wi-Fi

24/7 internet access available to support your academic and productive needs, provided through a subscription system.

Flexible Meal System

Each individual can decide whether to join a meal, offering both convenience and flexibility.

Study Space

A calm and disciplined shared environment that supports focused, distraction-free study.

Security

Access is strictly limited to registered members, and no one is allowed without prior permission.

Important Rules

To ensure a peaceful and productive environment for all students.

Strictly report in advance if you plan to return after 9:00 PM.

No outsiders allowed in rooms without permission.

Maintain silence to support focused study and avoid disturbing others.
